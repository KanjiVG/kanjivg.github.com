Frequently Asked Questions

Some issues and questions have come up more than once before, so it might save time before bringing something up if you check with this list first.

The characters in KanjiVG differ from printed styles

The characters in KanjiVG are based on handwritten Japanese styles. These differ from printed characters in various ways. When comparing KanjiVG to styles you can find on a computer, please be sure to compare them to kyokashotai (教科書体) or kaisho (楷書) styles rather than printed styles like Mincho or Gothic. See the references page for the exact details of the references used in creating the characters.
